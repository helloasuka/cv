Carla Nave

Ph.D. student / Human-Computer Interaction Researcher at iNOVA Media Lab and NOVALincs

UI/UX Designer

Web Developer

With a background in Informatics Engineering and a Post-graduation in Usability and HCI, Carla Nave worked in the industry for a couple of years before going back to academia, where she is currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Digital Media. Her thesis is focused on the design of a tool for affective self-report, and her main interests include HCI methods, Affective Computing, and Positive Computing. Besides research, she also does web development. Currently, she is exploring the JAMstack (JAM - JavaScript, APIs, and Markup) to build fast websites.
