With a background in Informatics Engineering and a Post-graduation in Usability and HCI, Carla Nave worked in the industry for a couple of years before going back to academia, where she is currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Digital Media. Her thesis is focused on the design and development of a tool for affective self-report, and her main interests include HCI methods, Affective Computing, and Positive Computing. Aside from the PhD, she likes to run other projects, from apps to events.
