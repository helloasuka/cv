Carla Nave

Ph.D. student / Researcher at NOVALincs and iNOVA Media Lab

Based in Lisbon, Portugal

With a background in Informatics Engineering and a Post-graduation in Usability and HCI, Carla Nave worked in the industry for a couple of years before going back to academia, where she is currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Digital Media. Her thesis is focused on the design of a tool for affective self-report, and her main interests include HCI methods, Affective Computing, and Positive Computing. Apart from research, she also likes to organize events (Reboot festival, Digital Media meetups) and dabs in web development (HTML, CSS, Javascript, React, Gatsby).
